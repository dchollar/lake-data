Pipe and North Pipe Lakes Protection and Rehabilitation District Privacy Policy

Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

We are the sole owners of the information collected on this site. We only have access to/collect information that you voluntarily give us via email, forms, blogs, or another direct contact from you. We will not sell or rent this information to anyone.

We will use your information via cookies stored on your computer to respond to you. We may share your information with third-party vendors to fulfill your request or to work together with Pipe and North Pipe Lakes Protection and Rehabilitation District and their partners.

Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about specials, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy.

Lead Details

We will use your information to contact you about our services and products.

Your Access to and Control over Information

You may opt out of any future contacts from us at any time. You can do the following at any time by contacting us via the email address or phone number given on our website. Information that we can offer to you upon contact with us:

  • See what data we have about you, if any.
  • Revise/correct any data we have about you.
  • Delete any data we have about you.
  • Express any concern you have about our use of your data.

Updates

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time and all updates will be posted on this page.
